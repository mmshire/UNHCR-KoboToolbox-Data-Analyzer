KoBoToolbox Survey Analyzer
This tool was developed by the Information Management Unit (IMU) in Bangladesh to streamline recurrent data analysis for surveys hosted on the KoBoToolbox platform. It supports reproducible analysis workflows — upload your XLS form and data export once, and get instant frequency tables, indicator breakdowns, disaggregation by gender, camp, district, or any categorical variable, all styled to UNHCR data visualization standards.
📋 Form structure explorer 📊 Frequency analysis 🔢 Numeric indicators 🔀 Disaggregation layers 🌙 Dark mode 🔁 Reproducible
